# Guided construction record

The source response is stored at `model/construction-response.json`. It contains one response for every applicable question in the eleven-stage catalogue, decision state, provenance and review outcome.

## Stage disposition

| Stage | Result | Material design choice |
|---|---|---|
| Purpose and boundary | Complete | Essential-service access with explicit exclusions |
| Authority and legitimacy | Review-qualified | Authority is illustrative and must be replaced by adopters |
| Roles and responsibilities | Complete | Assessment, operation and appeal functions are separated |
| Participation and lifecycle | Complete | Admission plus ongoing surveillance |
| Assurance | Complete | AL-2 baseline, AL-3 high risk, critical floors |
| Conformance | Complete | Accredited independent assessment |
| Technology and registries | Profile-controlled | Technology-neutral core with bounded dependency profiles |
| Rights and remedy | Complete | Notice, challenge, independent appeal and executable remedies |
| Risk | Complete | Seven priority risks and three emergency scenarios |
| Recognition | Complete | Conditional equivalence with immediate suspension triggers |
| Maintenance | Complete | Annual and event-triggered review with migration duties |

## Deliberate unresolved item

The source of legal authority is recorded as `review_required`. This is intentional. A reusable example may demonstrate the structure of authority evidence, but it cannot invent the authority needed by an actual jurisdiction or institution.
