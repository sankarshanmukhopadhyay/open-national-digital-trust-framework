# Credential Profile

Every national or sector credential profile MUST define:

- schema and semantic vocabulary;
- issuer eligibility;
- subject and holder binding;
- proof format and cryptographic suite;
- issuance and presentation protocols;
- status and lifecycle handling;
- privacy and disclosure model;
- assurance level;
- evidence and retention obligations;
- accessibility and offline behaviour;
- test vectors and conformance requirements.

Credential validity is distinct from credential sufficiency. A cryptographically valid credential may still be unsuitable for a relying party's purpose.
