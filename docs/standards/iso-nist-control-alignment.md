# Informative security and risk control alignment

ONDTF control families may be mapped to ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 27002 and NIST SP 800-53. The relationship is many-to-many. ONDTF controls focus on national digital trust outcomes, including authority integrity, evidence sufficiency, effect admission and redress, while external catalogues have broader information-security scopes.

## Mapping principles

1. map outcomes, not labels alone;
2. preserve edition and clause identifiers;
3. state gaps and profile dependencies;
4. distinguish design evidence from operational effectiveness;
5. avoid certification language unless an accredited assessment has occurred;
6. review mappings when external standards or ONDTF controls change.

Initial crosswalk artefacts are maintained under `model/references/` and are intentionally incomplete where clause-level licensed text cannot be reproduced.
