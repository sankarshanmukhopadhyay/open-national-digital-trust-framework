# Foundations

This section defines the purpose, boundaries, principles, and framework-independence and optional compatibility relationships of ONDTF.

ONDTF separates four concerns that are frequently collapsed:

- **identity** establishes how an actor or object is distinguished;
- **authority** establishes why it may act;
- **assurance** establishes why evidence and controls may be relied upon;
- **governance** establishes who sets, changes, enforces, and remedies the rules.
