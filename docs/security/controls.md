# Baseline Controls

Conformant implementations SHOULD include:

- hardware-backed or equivalent key protection for high-assurance credentials;
- phishing-resistant authentication;
- key rotation, recovery and compromise procedures;
- algorithm and cryptographic-suite agility;
- replay protection, audience binding and nonce validation;
- short-lived authorisations for high-risk actions;
- privacy-preserving status checks;
- signed, versioned policy artefacts;
- separation of duties for critical governance actions;
- incident telemetry and rapid revocation propagation;
- secure software development and dependency provenance;
- independent penetration, privacy and governance reviews;
- tested business continuity and disaster recovery.
