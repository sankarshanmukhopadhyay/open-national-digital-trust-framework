# Security and Assurance

The ONDTF applies defence in depth across governance, technology and operations.

## Assurance dimensions

- identity proofing assurance;
- authenticator assurance;
- credential issuance assurance;
- holder or device binding assurance;
- verifier assurance;
- registry and trust-resolution assurance;
- policy and decision assurance;
- operational assurance;
- governance assurance.

Assurance MUST be contextual. A high-assurance identity credential does not automatically create high assurance that a delegated transaction is authorised or safe.

## Assurance levels

A deployment MAY define four levels:

- **ONDTF-A1:** low impact, basic controls;
- **ONDTF-A2:** moderate impact, verified actors and managed operations;
- **ONDTF-A3:** high impact, strong binding, independent assessment and rapid status propagation;
- **ONDTF-A4:** critical impact, hardened infrastructure, dual control, continuous monitoring and formal incident coordination.
