# Framework administration

The Framework Administrator maintains the controlled operational expression of the Governing Authority's decisions. Administration does not confer a general power to create policy outside delegated authority.

## Responsibilities

- maintain authoritative framework and profile versions;
- operate controlled-document and change processes;
- publish notices, effective dates and migration windows;
- maintain role, delegation, provider and dependency registers;
- coordinate consultations and issue disposition;
- preserve decision and publication evidence;
- support implementation guidance without redefining normative obligations;
- report unresolved risks and overdue reviews to the Governing Authority.

## Administrative neutrality

Where the Administrator also operates infrastructure or provides trust services, the adoption must disclose the overlap and implement conflict controls. Administrative control over publication must not be used to suppress adverse assessment, appeal or oversight findings.
