# Redress and Accountability

Redress is a core trust service, not an afterthought.

A conformant trust scheme MUST define:

- who may complain or appeal;
- accessible channels, including assisted and offline routes;
- evidence available to affected parties;
- response and resolution times;
- emergency suspension and harm-containment measures;
- independent escalation;
- correction of credentials, registries and downstream decisions;
- restitution or compensation where applicable;
- publication of aggregate outcomes.

Automated decisions SHOULD produce understandable reason codes and a traceable policy version. High-impact decisions MUST support meaningful human review.
