# Known limitations

ONDTF remains a draft framework. The following limitations are intentional and must remain visible through v0.5.0.

- The framework has not yet accumulated broad independent implementation evidence.
- External standards mappings are informative unless a profile expressly selects a dependency.
- Jurisdiction profiles are implementation aids and are not legal advice.
- Quantitative risk scales support repeatability but do not eliminate professional judgement or uncertainty.
- Reference schemas and fixtures demonstrate expected structure; they are not production software.
- Cross-border recognition depends on legal, institutional and operational arrangements outside the technical framework.
- Cryptographic and protocol choices remain profile-dependent.
- Conformance claims require evidence and assessment under an adopted scheme; repository validation alone is not conformance.

Limitations SHOULD be converted into tracked issues when they can be resolved through implementation, review or testing.
