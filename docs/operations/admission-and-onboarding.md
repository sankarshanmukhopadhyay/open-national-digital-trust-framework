# Admission and onboarding

Admission establishes eligibility to participate. It does not establish unrestricted authority or permanent good standing.

## Admission decision

The admission authority MUST verify the applicant's legal existence or recognised standing, authority to apply, beneficial ownership or control where relevant, competence, operational capability, security and privacy posture, financial and continuity capacity, declared dependencies and conflicts of interest.

A decision MUST identify the approved provider, service scope, assurance level, jurisdiction, conditions, validity period, surveillance plan and appeal route.

## Activation

Activation occurs only after the approval decision, required agreements, register publication, endpoint validation, incident-contact confirmation and any pre-operational tests are complete. The activation time MUST be recorded and propagated to authoritative status services.

## Rejection and resubmission

A rejected applicant MUST receive reasons sufficient to understand the decision, subject to lawful confidentiality limits. The scheme MUST define review, appeal and resubmission conditions.
