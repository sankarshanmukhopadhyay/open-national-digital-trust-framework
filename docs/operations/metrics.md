# Metrics and Service Levels

National and sector operators SHOULD publish metrics for:

- registry availability and latency;
- status propagation time;
- credential issuance and verification success;
- false acceptance and false rejection rates where applicable;
- fraud and incident rates;
- wallet recovery outcomes;
- accessibility and assisted-channel use;
- complaint and appeal resolution time;
- cross-sector interoperability test pass rate;
- concentration and dependency risk;
- privacy incidents and excessive-data requests.

Metrics MUST be interpreted with safeguards against perverse incentives. Faster processing is not sufficient if it weakens due process, privacy or accuracy.
