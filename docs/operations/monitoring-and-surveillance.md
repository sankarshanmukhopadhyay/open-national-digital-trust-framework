# Monitoring and surveillance

Continued recognition depends on evidence that obligations remain satisfied after admission.

## Surveillance sources

Surveillance MAY combine scheduled assessment, automated telemetry, incident reports, complaints, status anomalies, audit evidence, material-change notifications, dependency intelligence and thematic reviews.

The scheme MUST define risk-based frequency, evidence access, sampling, escalation, provider response time and independence safeguards. Monitoring that affects individuals MUST observe purpose limitation, proportionality and privacy obligations.

## Outcomes

Surveillance may produce no action, observation, corrective action, increased monitoring, scope restriction, suspension, reassessment or withdrawal. Every consequential outcome MUST be evidence-bearing and reviewable.
