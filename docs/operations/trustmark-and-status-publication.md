# Trustmark and status publication

A trustmark or register entry is a scoped status claim, not a general endorsement.

Published status MUST identify the provider, authorised service, profile, assurance or conformance basis, issuing authority, effective and expiry times, restrictions, current state and authoritative verification location. A visual mark MUST resolve to equivalent machine-verifiable status.

The scheme MUST define controls against misleading display, stale caching, unauthorised copying and continued use after suspension or withdrawal.
