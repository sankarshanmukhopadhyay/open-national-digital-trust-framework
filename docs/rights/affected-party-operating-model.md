# Affected-party operating model

An affected party is a person or organisation whose rights, interests, opportunities, obligations or safety may be materially altered by a trust decision, even when that party is not the principal, provider, holder, verifier or contractual participant.

The operating model requires frameworks to identify affected-party classes, likely harms, notice routes, participation points, representation, accessibility needs, escalation paths and remedies. Responsibilities must be allocated across the provider, relying party, framework administrator, supervisor and independent review body rather than left to a generic support channel.
