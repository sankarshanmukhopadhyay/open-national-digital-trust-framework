# Notice and explanation

A DTF must define when notice is required, who provides it, what information it contains and how an affected party can act on it. Notice should identify the material decision, responsible parties, evidence categories, applicable rules, consequence, challenge route, time limits and accessible alternatives.

Explanation must be sufficient to contest the outcome. Merely stating that a verification failed or a policy rule was applied is inadequate when the party cannot determine what evidence, authority or status was decisive.
