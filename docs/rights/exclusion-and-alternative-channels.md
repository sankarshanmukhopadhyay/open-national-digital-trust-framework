# Exclusion and alternative channels

A DTF must specify what happens when a person cannot obtain, present or verify the preferred evidence. Alternative channels should preserve the underlying assurance objective without requiring identical technology or creating punitive delay.

Profiles must examine exclusion caused by documentation gaps, biometric failure, connectivity, disability, name or status mismatch, guardianship, displacement and system error. Alternative routes require clear authority, fraud controls, escalation and equivalent access to review and remedy.
