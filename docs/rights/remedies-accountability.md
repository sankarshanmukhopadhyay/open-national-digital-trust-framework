# Remedies and accountability

Remedy aims to stop continuing harm, correct records or status, reverse or mitigate effects, compensate where applicable, restore access or standing, sanction misconduct, improve controls, and preserve evidence of action taken.

A remedy record SHOULD identify the finding, responsible parties, affected parties, action, deadline, verification evidence, residual harm, and closure authority. Systemic findings SHALL feed back into risk, controls, policy, training, and assurance.
