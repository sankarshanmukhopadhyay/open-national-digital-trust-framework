# Appeal and independent review

Appeal provides review beyond the original decision-maker. Independence must be assessed against organisational control, financial incentives, prior involvement and the review body's power to obtain evidence and order corrective action.

A profile must define appeal eligibility, filing periods, suspension of harmful effects, review standard, evidence access, decision deadlines, publication rules and further escalation to regulators, courts or other competent bodies.
