# Affected parties and participation

An affected party is any person, organisation, community, or public interest materially exposed to an ONDTF-enabled decision or effect. The set may extend beyond the principal, delegate, issuer, holder, verifier, operator, or contracting parties.

Governance SHOULD provide mechanisms to identify foreseeable affected groups, include relevant interests in policy design, protect vulnerable parties, and enable representation where direct participation is impractical.
