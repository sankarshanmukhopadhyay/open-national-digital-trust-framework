# Operating model overview

An ONDTF operating model must establish who is authorised to govern, who performs operational functions, how those functions are assured, how decisions are reviewed, and how the framework remains sustainable.

## Design principles

- public purpose precedes technology selection;
- accountability remains identifiable across delegated and outsourced operations;
- rule-making, operation, assurance, and appeal should be separated where concentration creates unacceptable risk;
- national coordination should coexist with sector competence and regional autonomy;
- affected-party interests must be represented in governance and review;
- operational evidence must support both real-time decisions and later accountability;
- emergency powers must not become permanent default powers;
- adoption should proceed through explicit maturity stages.
