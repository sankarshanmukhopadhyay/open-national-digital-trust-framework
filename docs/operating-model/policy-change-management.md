# Policy and change management

Material policy changes can alter eligibility, authority, evidence requirements, costs, and rights. They therefore require governance comparable to changes in critical infrastructure.

## Standard change path

```mermaid
flowchart LR
  N[Need identified] --> IA[Impact assessment]
  IA --> C[Consultation]
  C --> D[Authorised decision]
  D --> P[Publication]
  P --> T[Transition period]
  T --> E[Effective operation]
  E --> R[Post-implementation review]
```

A material change should include rationale, affected parties, legal basis, risk and rights impacts, interoperability effects, migration plan, effective date, rollback or contingency plan, and review arrangements.

## Emergency changes

Emergency changes may shorten consultation and transition, but must identify a trigger, authorised decision-maker, bounded scope, expiry, notification duty, evidence record, and retrospective review.
