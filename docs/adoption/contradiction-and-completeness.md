# Contradiction and completeness checks

The construction system must challenge internally inconsistent or unsupported choices instead of generating polished prose around them.

## Illustrative contradictions

- a binding framework with no identified source of authority;
- independent assurance combined with provider-controlled final assessment;
- high assurance with no evidence, freshness, or surveillance model;
- one institution controlling governance, accreditation, assessment, and appeal without mitigation;
- authoritative registry status with no outage or stale-data semantics;
- cross-border recognition with no suspension, liability, or remedy pathway;
- automated adverse effects with no explanation, challenge, or independent review;
- mandatory digital access with no accessible or alternative channel.

## Severity

- **warning**: requires attention but may not block drafting;
- **material**: blocks approval of the affected section;
- **critical**: blocks profile activation.

Contradiction rules are represented in `model/adoption/contradiction-rules.yaml`. Completeness gates are represented in `model/adoption/completeness-gates.yaml`.
