# Workshop facilitation guide

Guided construction is suitable for a sequence of facilitated workshops involving policy, legal, governance, technical, security, privacy, accessibility, operational, assurance, and affected-party perspectives.

## Recommended workshop pattern

1. circulate the current stage questions and evidence requests;
2. identify decision owner, advisers, and affected groups;
3. record candidate answers and alternatives;
4. test authority, risk, operational feasibility, and rights consequences;
5. assign decision state and review gates;
6. capture dissent, unresolved issues, and required evidence;
7. approve only through the defined governance route.

The facilitator must not convert absence of objection into authority or consensus. Material disagreement should be represented in the decision register and escalated according to the governance model.
