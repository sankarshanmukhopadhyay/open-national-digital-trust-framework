# Assurance Levels

ONDTF does not define one universal trust score. A profile should select assurance requirements per decision and risk.

| Level | Suitable use | Minimum characteristics |
|---|---|---|
| A0 | Informational, non-consequential | Unverified source; no reliance claim |
| A1 | Low-risk participation | Basic provenance, status, and integrity checks |
| A2 | Material or regulated decisions | Verified authority, policy controls, current status, auditable evidence |
| A3 | High-impact or systemic decisions | Independent assessment, continuous monitoring, strong separation of duties, tested recovery and redress |

Profiles may rename or extend levels but must publish a mapping to these baseline characteristics.
