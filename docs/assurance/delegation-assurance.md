# Delegation assurance

Delegation assurance evaluates whether authority has been validly passed from a principal to a delegate and remains usable for the proposed action. The evaluation covers the delegator's own authority, permitted redelegation, scope, purpose, duration, conditions, revocation, acceptance and traceability.

A delegation conclusion must identify the principal, delegate, authority source, authorised actions, affected resources, constraints, status source and freshness requirement. A technically valid delegation artefact does not cure an invalid original mandate.
