# Authority assurance

Authority assurance evaluates whether an actor is entitled to perform a particular act in a particular context. It requires evidence of the authority source, scope, conditions, exclusions, validity period, revocation status and the decision rule that interprets those facts.

Authority may arise from law, office, contract, organisational policy, guardianship, consent or another recognised source. Authentic evidence of an office or role is insufficient where the requested act falls outside that authority.
