# Remedy readiness assurance

Remedy readiness assurance evaluates whether an affected party can discover an adverse outcome, understand the responsible decision path, challenge relevant evidence, obtain independent review and receive an effective remedy.

This dimension prevents assurance from ending at technical correctness or organisational certification. Required evidence includes notice routes, case intake, accessible channels, record access, review independence, decision time limits, remedy powers, completion evidence and learning from recurring failures.
