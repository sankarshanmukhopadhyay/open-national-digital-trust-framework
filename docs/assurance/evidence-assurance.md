# Evidence assurance

Evidence assurance evaluates whether information used in a trust decision is authentic, attributable, relevant, sufficient, current and reproducible. Evidence quality depends on provenance, collection method, integrity protection, semantic fitness, independence and the ability to inspect contrary or missing evidence.

Profiles must define minimum evidence classes, acceptable sources, freshness, retention and uncertainty treatment. A valid signature establishes integrity and attribution within its limits; it does not establish truth, relevance or sufficient authority.
