# Privacy assurance

Privacy assurance evaluates whether information collection, disclosure, correlation, retention and secondary use are proportionate to the declared purpose and governed by effective controls. It includes minimisation, selective disclosure, unlinkability, query privacy, consent where relevant, lawful basis and rights execution.

Privacy assurance must be evidenced through architecture, implementation behaviour and operational practice. A privacy-preserving protocol cannot compensate for excessive purpose, opaque decision-making or unjustified retention.
