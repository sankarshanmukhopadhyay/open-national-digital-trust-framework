# Health and Care Profile

Use cases include professional licences, patient identity, consent and care-authority credentials, insurance eligibility, laboratory reports, prescriptions and emergency-access mandates.

The profile MUST distinguish consent from lawful authority and clinical necessity. Emergency access MUST be exceptional, logged, reviewable and proportionate. Sensitive health claims SHOULD use selective disclosure and strong anti-correlation controls.
