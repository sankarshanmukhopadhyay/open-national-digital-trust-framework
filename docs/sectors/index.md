# Multi-sector Profiles

Sector profiles inherit the national architecture and add domain-specific rules. A sector profile may be stricter than the national baseline but SHOULD NOT break interoperability without a documented necessity.

## Initial sectors

- financial services;
- health and care;
- education and skills;
- employment and social protection;
- travel, transport and hospitality;
- telecommunications;
- trade, supply chains and corporate identity;
- land, property and public records;
- justice and regulated professions;
- energy and critical infrastructure.

Each profile MUST define authorities, credential types, assurance levels, data-minimisation rules, status semantics, verifier obligations, liability, evidence and redress.
