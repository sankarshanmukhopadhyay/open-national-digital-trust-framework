# Model Risk Register

| Risk | Cause | Impact | Baseline treatment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Governance capture | concentrated decision rights | exclusion or preferential access | independent oversight, transparency, appeal |
| Registry compromise | weak operational controls | false trust decisions | signed records, multi-party control, monitoring |
| Format fragmentation | incompatible profiles | vendor lock-in | mandatory profiles and conformance testing |
| Excessive disclosure | verifier over-collection | privacy harm | request policies, selective disclosure, audits |
| Stale revocation | delayed status propagation | acceptance of invalid credentials | service levels, cache controls, emergency channels |
| Agent overreach | broad delegation | unauthorised action | bounded mandates, runtime enforcement, evidence |
| Digital exclusion | device or literacy barriers | denial of essential services | assisted, offline and non-digital channels |
