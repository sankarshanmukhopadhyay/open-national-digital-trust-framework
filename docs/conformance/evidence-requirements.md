# Evidence requirements

Evidence MUST be relevant, authentic, sufficiently current, traceable to the assessed object and adequate for the requirement being evaluated.

Each evidence item SHOULD record provenance, collector, collection time, applicable requirement, method, integrity protection, confidentiality classification, validity period and limitations. Sampling and reliance on third-party evidence MUST be justified.

Absence of evidence is not evidence of conformance. Where a requirement is not applicable, the rationale and approving authority MUST be recorded.
