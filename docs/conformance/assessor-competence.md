# Assessor competence and independence

The assessment scheme MUST define competence for the legal, governance, technical, security, privacy, accessibility and operational domains within scope.

Assessors MUST disclose conflicts, preserve confidentiality, use controlled methods, record evidence and findings, and avoid assessing work for which they exercised incompatible design or decision authority. The certification or approval decision SHOULD be made by a person or function independent of the assessment team.

Where accreditation is required, the Accreditation Authority MUST define recognition, surveillance, suspension and withdrawal of Conformity Assessment Bodies.
