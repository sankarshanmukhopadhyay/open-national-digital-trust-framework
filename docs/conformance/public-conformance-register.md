# Public conformance register

A public register enables relying parties to distinguish current, scoped claims from expired or misleading assertions.

The register MUST publish the claimant, assessed object, scheme and profile, claim type, scope, decision authority, assessor or certification body where disclosure is permitted, issue and expiry dates, current state, restrictions and authoritative status endpoint.

The register MUST preserve enough historical status to establish whether a claim was active at a relevant time.
