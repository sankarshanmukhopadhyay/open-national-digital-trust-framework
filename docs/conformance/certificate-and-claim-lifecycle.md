# Certificate and claim lifecycle

A conformance claim may be draft, issued, active, restricted, suspended, expired, withdrawn or superseded. Status transitions MUST be authoritative, timestamped and linked to the decision record.

A certificate MUST NOT outlive its assessed object, applicable profile or critical evidence. Material changes may require amendment, reassessment or replacement. Historical status MUST remain available for audit and dispute resolution subject to lawful retention limits.
