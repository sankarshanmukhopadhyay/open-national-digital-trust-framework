# Surveillance and reassessment

Surveillance tests whether the basis of a conformance claim remains valid. Frequency and depth MUST reflect risk, assurance level, incident history, material changes, dependency volatility and previous findings.

Reassessment MUST occur before expiry and after changes designated by the scheme as reassessment triggers. The scheme MAY use continuous evidence, but automated signals must not replace accountable decision making for consequential status changes.
