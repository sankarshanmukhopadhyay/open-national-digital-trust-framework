# Appeals and complaints

A claimant MUST be able to appeal an adverse assessment or certification decision. Participants and affected parties MUST be able to complain about assessor conduct, misleading claims, inaccessible processes or failures to enforce the scheme.

Appeal review MUST be independent of the original decision to the extent practicable. Procedures MUST define standing, submission channels, time limits, evidence access, confidentiality, interim measures, reasons, further review and remedy.
