# Nonconformity and remediation

The scheme MUST classify nonconformities by consequence and urgency. A finding record MUST state the violated requirement, evidence, affected scope, risk, containment, corrective action, responsible owner and due date.

Remediation closes only when corrective action is implemented and effectiveness is verified. Serious or repeated nonconformity may require restriction, suspension or withdrawal even while remediation is underway.
