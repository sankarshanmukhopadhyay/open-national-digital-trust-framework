# Assessment scheme

An assessment scheme defines how conformance is evaluated and maintained.

## Required scheme elements

- scheme owner and authority source;
- assessed objects and eligible claimants;
- applicable requirements and profiles;
- assessment methods and sampling;
- assessor competence and independence;
- evidence sufficiency and retention;
- decision authority and separation from assessment;
- validity, surveillance and reassessment;
- nonconformity grading and remediation;
- suspension, withdrawal and public status;
- complaints and appeals;
- change control and transition periods.

The machine-readable scheme in `model/conformance/assessment-scheme.yaml` exposes fields that the Guided Framework Construction flow can populate from adopter decisions.
